NATURAL PEST CONTROL

Botanical Pesticides:

Dashparni Ark:
Made from leaves of 10 plants (neem, papaya, pomegranate, etc.)
Preparation:
- Chop leaves of 10 different plants
- Add cow urine and water
- Ferment for 15-20 days
- Dilute 10% for spraying

Neem Oil Spray:
- Mix 5 ml neem oil per liter of water
- Add 1 ml liquid soap as emulsifier
- Effective against aphids, mites, whiteflies
- Apply early morning or late evening

Trap Methods:
- Yellow Pheromone traps: Attract fruit flies
- Light traps: Catch night-flying moths
- Sticky traps: Monitor pest populations

Important Guidelines:
- Avoid spraying chemicals during flowering stage
- Protect honeybees (essential for pollination)
- Use biological control agents (Trichogramma, ladybird beetles)
- Practice crop rotation to break pest cycles
