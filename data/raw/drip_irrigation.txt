DRIP IRRIGATION SYSTEM

Benefits:
- Reduces water wastage by 40-60%
- Increases yield by 20-30%
- Prevents weed growth
- Reduces fertilizer use by 30% through fertigation
- Suitable for horticulture crops

Crops Benefited:
- Horticulture: Pomegranate, grapes, banana, mango
- Vegetables: Tomato, brinjal, chili, capsicum
- Cash crops: Sugarcane, cotton
- Plantation: Tea, coffee

Installation Components:
1. Water source (well, pond, tank)
2. Pump unit with filter
3. Mainline and submain pipes
4. Drip lines with emitters
5. Fertigation tank (Venturi system)

Government Subsidies:
- Small farmers: 70-80% subsidy
- Medium farmers: 50-60% subsidy
- Large farmers: 40-50% subsidy
Apply through State Agriculture Department

Spacing Guidelines:
- Drip spacing: 30-50 cm for vegetables
- Lateral spacing: 1-1.5 m for row crops
- Emitter flow rate: 2-4 liters per hour
