WEATHER SAFETY MEASURES

Hailstorm Protection:
- Cover sensitive vegetable crops with plastic nets or shade nets
- Install hail nets for orchard crops
- Keep harvested produce in covered storage
- Apply stress-tolerant chemicals (potassium nitrate, brassinosteroids)
- Government compensation available through crop insurance

Heavy Rain Management:
- Ensure proper field drainage (construct drainage channels)
- Avoid waterlogging (critical for root crops)
- Apply balanced fertilizers after heavy rain
- Use raised beds for vegetable cultivation
- Monitor for fungal diseases (apply preventive fungicides)

Drought Management:
- Adopt mulching to conserve soil moisture
- Use drought-tolerant varieties
- Practice micro-irrigation (drip/sprinkler)
- Maintain organic matter in soil (water holding capacity)
- Plan cropping patterns based on rainfall forecast

Heat Wave Precautions:
- Provide shade to young plants
- Increase irrigation frequency
- Apply kaolin clay spray to reduce sunburn
- Harvest during cooler hours (early morning)
