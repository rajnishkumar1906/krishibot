**Best Mobile Apps for Indian Farmers (Free/Paid)**

1. **Kisan Suvidha** (Govt of India)
   - Weather forecast, market prices (MSP, mandi rates), plant protection advisories.
   - Farmer’s call centre, dealers, radio programmes.
   - Available in Hindi, English, and many regional languages.

2. **Plantix** (AI pest/disease diagnosis)
   - Take photo of leaf/fruit – AI identifies disease/pest and suggests remedy.
   - Also gives nutrient deficiency detection.
   - Free, works offline after initial download.
   - Languages: Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Marathi, English.

3. **CropIn** (Farm management)
   - Record farm activities (sowing, spray, harvest), get reminders.
   - Satellite-based crop health alerts.
   - Free for small farmers (limited features); paid for commercial farms.

4. **Pusa Krishi** (ICAR-IARI)
   - Weather-based advisories for Delhi region, crop varieties, pest management.
   - Also has agri-calculator (seed rate, fertilizer).

5. **eNAM** (National Agriculture Market)
   - Check real-time prices of 200+ commodities in 1000+ mandis.
   - Trade online with buyer after registering with mandi.

6. **Kisan Rath** (Transportation)
   - Connect with truckers for moving produce from farm to mandi.

7. **IFFCO BAZAR** (Inputs & Advisory)
   - Buy certified seeds, fertilizers, pesticides at MRP.
   - Also has agronomy advisory and weather forecast.

How to Get Started?
- Most apps available on Google Play Store.
- Need smartphone with 4G connection (₹1500-2000 phone works).
- Use local language – enable Hindi/regional language in phone settings.

Tips:
- Cross-check AI diagnosis with local KVK expert before spraying.
- Use multiple apps: Plantix for diagnosis, Kisan Suvidha for weather/mandi, eNAM for price comparison.
