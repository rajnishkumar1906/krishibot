MARKET INFORMATION SYSTEM

Minimum Support Price (MSP):
Government guaranteed prices for 23 crops:
- Paddy (common): ₹2183/quintal (2025)
- Wheat: ₹2275/quintal
- Cotton (medium): ₹7020/quintal
- Tur/Arhar: ₹7550/quintal
- Groundnut: ₹6410/quintal

e-NAM (National Agriculture Market):
- Online trading platform connecting 1000+ mandis
- Check real-time prices: https://enam.gov.in
- Features:
  - Transparent bidding process
  - Direct payment to farmers
  - Quality certification
  - Reduced middlemen involvement

Marketing Tips:
- Check prices in multiple mandis before selling
- Avoid distress selling during harvest
- Consider storage (warehouse receipt system)
- Join Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) for collective bargaining
- Use government procurement centers during MSP operations

APMC Mandi Charges:
- Market fee: 0.5-2% of produce value
- Commission charges: 1-2% (if using commission agent)
- Weighing, loading, and handling charges
