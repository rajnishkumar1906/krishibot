ORGANIC FERTILIZERS GUIDE

Jeevamrut:
Jeevamrut is a powerful organic fertilizer made from:
- Cow dung: 10 kg
- Cow urine: 10 liters
- Jaggery: 1 kg
- Pulse flour (besan): 1 kg
- Water: 200 liters
Mix all ingredients and let ferment for 5-7 days. Apply 200 liters per acre.

Vermicompost:
Uses earthworms (Eisenia fetida) to convert organic waste into nutrient-rich compost:
- Nutrient content: 1.5-2% N, 1-1.5% P, 0.5-1% K
- Application rate: 2-3 tons per hectare
- Improves soil structure and water holding capacity

Panchagavya:
Mixture of 5 cow products:
- Cow dung, urine, milk, curd, ghee
- Benefits: Growth promoter, disease resistance
- Dilute 3% in water for foliar spray

Benefits of Organic Fertilizers:
- Reduce dependency on chemical urea
- Improve soil microbial activity
- Enhance nutrient use efficiency
- Long-term soil health improvement
