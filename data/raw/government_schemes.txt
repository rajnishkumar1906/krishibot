GOVERNMENT SCHEMES FOR FARMERS

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi):
- Direct income support of ₹6000 per year
- Paid in three installments (₹2000 each)
- Eligible: All landholding farmer families
- Registration through Common Service Centers (CSCs)
- Status check: pmkisan.gov.in

PM Fasal Bima Yojana:
- Crop insurance against natural disasters
- Premium rates:
  - Kharif crops: 1.5-2%
  - Rabi crops: 1.5%
  - Commercial crops: 5%
- Coverage: Pre-sowing to post-harvest
- Claim settlement within 15 days of harvesting

Kisan Credit Card (KCC):
- Access to low-interest loans (4-7%)
- Credit limit based on land holding
- Covers cultivation, post-harvest expenses
- Also provides accident insurance coverage
- Apply at any commercial bank, RRB, or cooperative bank

Other Important Schemes:
1. PM-KUSUM: Solar pumps for farmers (60% subsidy)
2. Agricultural Infrastructure Fund: ₹1 lakh crore fund for infrastructure
3. Soil Health Card Scheme: Free soil testing every 3 years
4. Neem Coated Urea Scheme: Subsidized neem-coated urea
5. National Mission on Natural Farming: Promotes organic practices

Application Process:
- Visit nearest CSC or Agriculture Department office
- Documents required: Land records, Aadhaar, bank account
- Online applications through respective scheme portals
